About Aadhaar Validator

Aadhaar Validator is an AI-based verification tool designed to authenticate and extract key information from Aadhaar card images. It leverages Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and pattern analysis to identify and validate Aadhaar numbers, names, and dates of birth accurately.

This project ensures data privacy and security, offering a reliable way to verify identity for government, corporate, and educational institutions.

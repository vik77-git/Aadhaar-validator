How to Use

  1. Click on “Scan Aadhaar” from the navigation bar.
  2. Upload a clear Aadhaar card image (.jpg or .png).
  3. Click on “Scan Aadhaar” to extract and validate data.
  4. Results will show the Aadhaar number, name, DOB, and photo.

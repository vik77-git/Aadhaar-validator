Instructions for Aadhaar Validation

  1. Click on “Start Scanning” on the Home page.
  2. Select a clear image of your Aadhaar card (JPG, JPEG, or PNG format).
  3. Ensure the text and photo on the card are visible and not blurred.
  4. Click “Scan Aadhaar” to begin verification.
  5. Wait for a few seconds while the system extracts and validates your details.
  6. Review the extracted Aadhaar number, name, and date of birth displayed on the screen.
